About Us

ELCC is dedicated to providing high-quality language training in English, French, Arabic, and Turkish. Our experienced international teachers conduct sessions online and, when needed, in-person. With years of experience, our educators bring the global classroom to you—right from the comfort of your home. Group classes are kept small (4–6 students) to ensure interactive and personalized learning experiences.
